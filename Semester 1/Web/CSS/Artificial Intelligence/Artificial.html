Understanding AI and Popular AI Platforms

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on creating systems that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence. This includes problem-solving, learning, language understanding, and decision-making.

About ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a conversational AI model developed by OpenAI. It utilizes deep learning to understand and generate human-like responses in various applications such as chatbots, content generation, and coding assistance.
